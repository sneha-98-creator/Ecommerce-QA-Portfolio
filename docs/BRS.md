# Business Requirement Specification (BRS)

## Purpose

The purpose of this document is to define the business requirements for the ShopEasy E-Commerce application.

---

## Business Goals

- Allow customers to create secure accounts.
- Enable secure authentication.
- Provide product browsing and search functionality.
- Support online shopping with secure payment processing.
- Enable users to track their orders.
- Maintain customer purchase history.
- Deliver a smooth and reliable shopping experience.

---

## Business Rules

- Email address must be unique.
- Password must meet security requirements.
- Users must log in before checkout.
- Payment is processed only after order validation.
- Inventory should be updated after successful payment.
- Orders can only be cancelled before shipment.

---

## Assumptions

- Users have internet connectivity.
- Payment gateway is available.
- Product inventory is maintained by the system.

---

## Dependencies

- Payment Gateway
- Email Service
- Inventory Management
- Shipping Service
